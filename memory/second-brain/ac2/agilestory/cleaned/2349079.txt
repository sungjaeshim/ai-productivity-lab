If we are software professionals then we care about our craft, and about our crafstmanship. We don't throw a bunch of code together and poke and prod it until it soft-of works. Rather we take care to make sure the code is well conceived, well written, well tested, and well documented. I wear a green wrist-band that says TEST FIRST as a symbol of this careful professional attitude. It represents a promise that I made to myself and my profession, that I will not be careless with my code.
