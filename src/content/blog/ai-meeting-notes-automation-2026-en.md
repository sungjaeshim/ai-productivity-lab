## Which Tool Should You Choose?

### For Budget-Conscious Users → Otter.ai
Most affordable paid plan ($8.33/mo) with solid transcription quality. Good for individual professionals.

### For Teams with Diverse Languages → Fireflies.ai
Best for multilingual teams with 60+ language support. Strong AI summaries and action item extraction.

120 minutes/month free is generous for light users. Upgrade only when needed.

1. **Real-time Transcription** — See text as people speak
